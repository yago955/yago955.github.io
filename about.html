About Me

Hello there! I'm a high school student with a strong passion for programming. It's like weaving digital wonders and bringing ideas to life through coding. But yeah, I won't geek out on you.

When I'm not immersed in coding projects, you'll find me gliding around on my skateboard. I enjoy perfecting cool tricks and having a bit of fun on wheels.

Photography is another of my interests- both analog and digital. Capturing moments and telling stories through the lens is a fascinating art for me. As a founding member of the camera club "Soilboys," I get to share my love for photography with other enthusiasts.

I also play bass for our band called "Razorblade". We make rock/metal music, performing a few gigs here and there with everyone being insanely talented on stage. Music allows me to connect with others and express myself in a whole different way.

So, that's me. A high schooler who codes, skates, snaps, and rocks! I'm on an exciting journey of exploration and discovery. Stay tuned for more as I navigate through my passions and embrace the adventures of being a teenager. Thank you for joining me on this ride!
